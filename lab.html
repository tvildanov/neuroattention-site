Инфраструктура данных и исследований, формирующая новые подходы к адаптации и развитию человека в технологическом мире

NeuroAttention Lab —

Это исследовательская платформа, которая изучает внимание, состояние и поведение человека через сочетание нейронауки, технологий и практических протоколов.

Платформа объединяет:

  • исследовательские методики
  • системы сбора и анализа данных
  • цифровые и физические среды
  • разработки на основе искусственного интеллекта

Цель

Создание среды и инструментов, которые улучшают качество жизни и расширяют функциональные возможности человека.

Current Research Programs

NeuroAttention Lab conducts independent pilot studies exploring attention, nervous system regulation, brain-body integration, and human adaptation in rapidly changing environments.

01

Human Adaptation

  • Attention training
  • Cognitive flexibility
  • Emotional self-regulation
  • Stress resilience

02

Brain–Body Integration

  • Mobility restoration
  • Sensorimotor coordination
  • Recovery support following injury, stroke, and traumatic brain injury
  • Brain-body communication and perception training

03

Neurodevelopmental & Neurological Observations

  • Tourette syndrome
  • Autism spectrum participants
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Alzheimer's-related cognitive support
  • Peripheral neuropathy

04

Social & Cognitive Systems

  • Human relationships
  • Family and social adaptation
  • Communication and group dynamics
  • Attention-based approaches to quality of life

All projects are conducted as independent pilot and observational programs. NeuroAttention Lab does not provide medical treatment and does not claim clinical outcomes.

Ценность данных

Платформа собирает и анализирует данные о внимании, состоянии и поведении человека. Это позволяет:

  • создавать персонализированные протоколы развития
  • повышать когнитивные и поведенческие показатели
  • обучать системы искусственного интеллекта
  • разрабатывать новые продукты и технологические решения

Technology Partnerships

NeuroAttention Lab is seeking long-term technology partners working in neurotechnology, biofeedback, cognitive assessment, AI-assisted cognition, and human adaptation systems.

Our goal is not to review devices. Our goal is to evaluate technologies that may become part of the future NeuroAttention XR Platform.

A

Areas of Interest

  • Neurofeedback & EEG
  • Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI)
  • Wearable cognitive monitoring
  • Biofeedback technologies
  • AI-assisted cognition
  • Human adaptation technologies

B

Partnership Opportunities

  • Device testing
  • Protocol development
  • Integration research
  • User experience evaluation
  • Long-term field observations

We believe the future of human adaptation will emerge through the integration of neuroscience, technology, and practical training systems.

Devices We Are Evaluating

NeuroAttention Lab is currently evaluating neurotechnology platforms and wearable systems for potential integration into the NeuroAttention XR ecosystem.

We have already tested Muse and gained valuable insights into the requirements of our research workflows. This experience helped us identify several key priorities, including participant tracking, protocol customization, session management, data export, and API accessibility.

A

Evaluation Criteria

  • Raw data access
  • API / SDK availability
  • Session management
  • Multi-user support
  • Data export capabilities
  • XR integration potential

B

Currently Evaluating

  • Neurosity
  • EMOTIV
  • OpenBCI
  • BrainBit
  • Muse
  • Sens.ai
  • Mendi

Our objective is to identify technologies that can support measurable improvements in attention, self-regulation, adaptation, and human performance while enabling future integration into the NeuroAttention XR Platform.
